Troubleshooting

Calculator Stuck On: calculating...

Reason: The server is either not responding or it is down

Calculator Gives: Attempted Division by 0

Reason: Double check you did not try to divide by 0

Calculator Gives: 2-variable equations must be entered in ax+by=c form

Please enter the equation in that form, ax + by = c, even if a or b is 1 or 0
